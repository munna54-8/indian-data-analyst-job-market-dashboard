Key findings

  • Skill SQL (11.7%) and Excel (10.3%) remain the top two fundamentals — still worth learning first.
  • GenAI RAG outranks Python and Power BI — a real, current differentiator most candidates aren't demonstrating yet.
  • Geography Bengaluru, Hyderabad, and Pune post within 8 jobs of each other — hiring is far less Bengaluru-centric than assumed.
  • Pay Mumbai and Chennai show the highest average disclosed ranges; Delhi the lowest.
  • Transparency Fewer than 1 in 5 postings disclose salary at all, making benchmarking hard for candidates.

Recommendation

For someone entering the Data Analyst job market in India today: prioritize SQL and Excel first — still the most-requested fundamentals — then build working familiarity with RAG/GenAI tools, which now outrank core Python skills in demand and remain a genuine differentiator. Don't over-index applications on Bengaluru alone; Hyderabad and Pune carry nearly identical hiring volume and likely less applicant competition.
